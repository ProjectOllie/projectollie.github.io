posted 18 hr 27 min ago by perculia
Patch 7.2 PTR work continues - Wowhead's database is now updated with the latest items, spells and more! Below we're previewing the new interesting items we've found. Check out our other recent post to read about our Artifact Calculator updates and a list of new golden traits!

There are four legendaries added in Patch 7.2 that require specific materials to craft. These legendaries do not have procs associated with them yet on the PTR - likely incomplete. They do show up in the "legendary" tab in the Adventure Journal loot section, alongside the other legendaries.
  •  Celumbra, the Night's Dichotomy:  Bolt of Shadowcloth,  Bolt of Starweave,  Felessence Legion-Flask, 65  Blood of Sargeras, 125  Imbued Silkweave,  Sharp Spritethorn,  Runic Catgut
  •  The Sentinel's Eternal Refuge:  Wisp-Touched Elderhide,  Felessence Legion-Flask, 65  Blood of Sargeras, 250  Stonehide Leather
  •  Vigilance Perch:  Prime Wardenscale,  Felessence Legion-Flask, 65  Blood of Sargeras, 250  Stormscale
  •  Rethu's Incessant Courage:  Hammer of Forgotten Heroes,  Felessence Legion-Flask, 65  Blood of Sargeras, 250  Demonsteel Bar
